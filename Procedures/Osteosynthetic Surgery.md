# Osteosynthetic Surgery

 

---

## Steps:

**1.** Anesthetic of your choice ([[anesthesia]])
**2.** [[Scalpel]]
**3.** [[Hemostat]]
**4.** [[Retractors]]
**5.** [[Surgical drill]]
**6.** [[Osteosynthesis implants]]
**7.** [[Sutures]]

Must be performed on every body part in order to treat [[bone death]].
