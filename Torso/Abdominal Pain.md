# Abdominal Pain

 

---

Abdominal pain is not lethal, and will only halve speed for 1 second every 3-6 seconds.

---
## Caused by:

- 80%)

## Causes:

- Half speed for 1 second every 3-6 seconds

## Treatments:

- Treating the causes
- Painkillers ([[anesthesia]])
