# Internal Bleeding

 

---

Internal bleeding will quickly drain the blood out of the patient, and must be dealt with quickly.

---
## Caused by:

- Trauma (gunshots, explosions, bite [[wounds]], lacerations)
- [[Liver damage]] (at 100%)
- [[Aortic rupture]]

## Causes:

- [[Blood loss]]
- [[Vomiting]] blood (at 50% or more)

## Treatments:

- [[Open & close surgery]]
- [[Combat stimulant]]
- [[Endovascular balloon]]
