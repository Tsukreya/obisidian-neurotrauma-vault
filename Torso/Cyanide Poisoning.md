# Cyanide Poisoning

 

---

## Caused by:
- [Cyanide](https://barotraumagame.com/wiki/Cyanide)


## Causes:
- Oxygen low
- [[Hypoxemia]]
- [[Neurotrauma]] (at 70% or more)

## Treatments:

- [Cyanide Antidote](https://barotraumagame.com/wiki/Cyanide_Antidote)
- [Stabilozine](https://barotraumagame.com/wiki/Stabilozine)
