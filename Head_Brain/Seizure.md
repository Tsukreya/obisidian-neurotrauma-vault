# Seizure

 

---

Seizures will cause you to ragdoll whenever they trigger.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Stroke]]
- [[Acidosis]] (60% or more)
- [[Alkalosis]] (60% or more)
- [[Withdrawal]] (alcohol [[withdrawal]], 50% or more)
- [[Radiation sickness]] (50% or more)
- [[Opiate overdose]] (50% or more)

## Causes:

- [[Unconsciousness]]
- [[Spasms]]

## Treatments:

- Treating the causes
