# Hypoventilation

 

---

Hypoventilation is normally harmless, and can usually be ignored. Hypoventilation and [[hyperventilation]] will cancel each other out.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Analgesia]] (20% or more)
- [[Anesthesia]] (40% or more) 

## Causes:

- [[Acidosis]]

## Treatments:

- Treating the causes
- Inducing [[adrenaline]])
