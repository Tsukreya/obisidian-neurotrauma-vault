# Irregular Heartbeat

 

---

Irregular heartbeat is when the heart is not beating at its normal rate and can lead to [[cardiac arrest]].

---
## Caused by:
- [[Fibrillation]]

## Causes:
- [[Cardiac arrest]] (at 20% or more)
- [[Hypotension]]

## Treatment:
- [[AED]]
- CPR
- [[Autopulse]]
- Treating underlying causes
