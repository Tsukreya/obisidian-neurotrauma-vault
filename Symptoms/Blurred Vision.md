# Blurred Vision

 

---

Blurred vision is a symptom, which means that it isn't fatal and doesn't cause any other afflictions. As the name suggests, this affliction will make your vision blurrier.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Hypotension]] (55% or less)
- [[Concussion]]
