# Leg Swelling

 

---

Leg swelling is a symptom, which means that it isn't fatal and doesn't cause any other afflictions. Cyberlegs will not swell.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Liver damage]] (40% or more)
- [[Kidney damage]] (60% or more)
- [[Heart damage]] (80% or more)
