# Confusion

 

---

Confusion is a symptom, which means that it isn't fatal and doesn't cause any other afflictions.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Acidosis]] (15% or more)
- [[Sepsis]] (40% or more)
- [[Hypotension]] (30% or less)
- [[Hypoxemia]] (50% or more)
- [[Withdrawal]] (alcohol [[withdrawal]], 80% or more)
- [[Concussion]]
