# Weakness

 

---

Weakness is a symptom, which means that it isn't fatal and doesn't cause any other afflictions. Weakness will decrease melee damage you deal by 80%.

---
## Caused by:

- [[Cardiac tamponade]] (30% or more)
- [[Blood loss]] (60% or more)
- [[Acidosis]] (35% or more)
