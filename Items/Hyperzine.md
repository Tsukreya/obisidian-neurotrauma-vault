# Hyperzine

 

---

### Medical skill check:50
A powerful stimulant

### Application success:
-6% [[internal damage]]
+9% chem addition
+9% [[neurotrauma]]
+15% psychosis

### Application failure:
-3% internal  damage
+18% chem addiction
+30% psychosis

In both cases, the chemical provides 400% Haste and 400% Vigor and reduces Chem [[withdrawal]] by 45%. The player also recovers from stuns 90% faster for 60 seconds.
