# Osteosynthesis Implants

 

---

### Medical skill check: 45
The sixth step in [[drilled bones]].

### Application success:
Cures all [[fractures]] on the limb the implants were used on, and uses up 25% of the implants.

### Application failure:
+5% bleeding
+5% [[internal damage]]
