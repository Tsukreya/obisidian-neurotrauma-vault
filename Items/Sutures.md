# Sutures

 

---

### Medical skill check: 30
The final step in **all** surgical procedures. Also very useful for curing most [[wounds]] & bleeding.

### Application success:
Removes all surgery related afflictions (such as [[drilled bones]])
-20% gunshot [[wounds]]
-20% lacerations
-20% bite [[wounds]]
-20% deep tissue injuries
-40% bleeding

### Application failure:
+6% [[internal damage]]
