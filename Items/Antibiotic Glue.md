# Antibiotic Glue

 

---

### Medical skill check: 55
A glue used to quickly seal bleeding [[wounds]] and treat [[burns]] on a single limb.

### Application success:
-100% bleeding
-30% [[burns]]

### Application failure:
-75% bleeding
-15% [[burns]]
50% chance to cause [[heart attack]]
