# Diving Knife

 

---

### Medical skill check: 30
In addition to the normal uses of a knife, they can be used to remove [[bandages]].

### Application success:
Removes [[bandages]] on the limb the shears were used on.

### Application failure:
+15% bleeding
+10% lacerations
